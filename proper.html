Proper Hand Washing

In the slideshow down below we show and describe how to properly wash your hands.

Step 1: Turn on the water to as warm as you can bear. The heat alone will kill most viruses and bacteria.

Step 2: Pump the soap into your hands and lather your entire hands with the soap

Step 3: Make sure to also wash your wrist and lower arms for further protection

Step 4: Rinse away all remaining soap

Step 5: Dry your hands with a single use paper towel when out in public. (When at home use a regular towel to prevent waste)
