Stay Home!

Yep, you have heard it a million times but it is true! If you have any medical issues or are over the age of 65 it is highly advised that you stay home whenever possible. To some this is a huge challenge, but I have written a list of my favorite quarentine activities!

1. Play board games with family.

2. Learn something new! What is the one thing you have always wanted to know how to do? Well now you have time!

3. Cook a fancy meal!

4. Try a new Video Game.

5. Start a personal journal. It can be passed down generations of a time you went through a pandemic.

6. Teach your dogs new tricks! (If you dont have a dog, now is the time to get one!)
